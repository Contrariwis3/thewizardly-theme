The Wizardly

notes from the late shift

The page begins here; the stars thin into the same night it is made of.

Built and dreamed. The two domes on the left are finished (solid). The great geodesic frame is skinned on its lower half and still open at the top, its unbuilt ribs drawn in light. A crane works beside it; the monorail runs off past the frame's edge, still being extended. The only gold is the few sparks, the needle's beacon, the apex, a couple of lit windows, so each one stays special. The glow lives on the dreamed line, breathing slowly, like a plan being held in mind.
