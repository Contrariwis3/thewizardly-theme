The Wizardly

notes from the late shift

The page begins here; the stars thin into the same night it is made of.

Built left, dreamed right. The left is dense and solid, finished domes with lit windows, a tower with its beacon, the established city, inhabited, warm. At center the great geodesic is skinned on its lower half and its full lattice is still open above, the frame being dreamed, apex lit. The monorail's track is built all the way across; its cars, pale brass, are still to be built, running right toward open sky where only a faint outline of what's next is sketched. Gold is rationed and lives on the left, where people are awake.
