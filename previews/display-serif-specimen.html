Recoleta — the verdict Latinotype · commercial · Basic family 8 styles, $79

Recoleta nails the brief's vibe better than anything OFL: warm, rounded, optimistic, exactly the friendly-encyclopedia / World's-Fair-brochure feel the seed reached for. License and price are not the obstacle; self-hosting the woff2 on static.thewizardly.com is within a one-time webfont license.

The real cost is not the $79

Recoleta is one of the most-used display serifs in premium brand design right now. For a site whose entire thesis is sincere, non-template distinctiveness, paying to land on the single most-saturated warm serif buys you more resemblance to every other 2025 brand page, not less. That fights the slop test directly.

My honest take

Spend the $79 only if, seeing it next to Fraunces here, its warmth is decisively better to your eye and you accept the familiarity. My prior: it won't clear that bar, because the drenched dark world, the amber glow, and the Marquee Initial are already doing the differentiation. The distinctive-and-defensible pick is an OFL face that isn't the reflex.
